Milton Glaser
1929-2020

An American graphic designer

Born on 26 June 1929, Glaser died exactly 91 years later on his birthday in New York, where he had lived all his life. Some of his best known work includes "I Love NY", and the psychedelic 1966 poster for Bob Dylan's Greatest Hits album.
